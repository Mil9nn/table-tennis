# Table Tennis Platform - Subscription Model

## Overview

This document outlines the subscription model for the Table Tennis Scoring & Tournament Management Platform. The model uses a **generous freemium approach** with **flexible payment options** (Lifetime, Annual, and Quarterly) designed to serve both individual players and tournament organizers.

---

## Subscription Tiers

### 🆓 FREE TIER
**Perfect for**: Casual players and hobbyists

**What's Included:**
- Complete profile with basic statistics
- Unlimited match participation
- Join unlimited tournaments
- Create and manage teams
- Basic match statistics (W/L, sets, points)
- Basic shot tracking (all 18+ shot types)
- View last 20 matches
- Basic head-to-head records
- Basic leaderboard access

**Tournament Organizing:**
- Create up to 2 tournaments per year
- Maximum 16 participants per tournament
- Round-robin format only
- Self-scoring (no multi-scorer feature)
- Basic tournament management tools

**Price:** FREE forever

---

### 🎯 PRO TIER
**Perfect for**: Serious players and regular tournament organizers

**Multiple payment options available:**
- **Lifetime:** $50 (one-time payment, yours forever)
- **Annual:** $4.50/year (best value for recurring billing)
- **3-Month:** $2.50/3 months (try it out for a season)

**Everything in Free, PLUS:**

**For Players:**
- **Advanced Analytics Dashboard**
  - Weaknesses analysis with zone heatmaps
  - Shot effectiveness breakdown (all shot types)
  - Performance trends over time
  - Detailed serve/receive statistics
  - Origin distance analysis
  - Sector performance metrics

- **Enhanced Statistics**
  - Unlimited match history (vs 20 for free)
  - Advanced head-to-head analytics
  - Tournament-specific performance tracking
  - Win/loss streak tracking with context
  - AI-generated performance insights

- **Data Exports**
  - Export match data to CSV
  - Export statistics to PDF
  - Download shot analysis data

**For Tournament Organizers:**
- Create up to 10 tournaments per year
- Maximum 50 participants per tournament
- All tournament formats (Round-robin, Knockout, Hybrid)
- Multi-Scorer feature (add up to 3 scorers)
- Export brackets and results (PDF/CSV)
- Advanced seeding options
- Team tournaments (all formats)

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
A: Your data is never deleted. If you cancel a subscription, you'll keep Pro features until the end of your billing period, then return to Free tier. Pro features become read-only or hidden. Lifetime purchases never expire.

**Q: What's the difference between Lifetime, Annual, and 3-Month?**
A: All three options include the exact same Pro tier features. Lifetime is a one-time payment with access forever. Annual and 3-Month are recurring subscriptions that you can cancel anytime.

**Q: Can I switch between payment options?**
A: Yes! You can upgrade from any subscription to Lifetime at any time. For switching between Annual and 3-Month subscriptions, the change takes effect at your next renewal date.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans, including Lifetime purchases.

### For Players

**Q: What analytics are included in Pro?**
A: Pro includes weaknesses analysis, shot effectiveness, performance trends, serve/receive stats, and AI-generated insights.

**Q: Can I export my match data?**
A: Yes! Pro tier users (all payment options) can export match data to CSV and statistics reports to PDF.

### For Tournament Organizers

**Q: What counts as an "active tournament"?**
A: Tournaments in Draft, Upcoming, or In Progress status count toward your limit. Completed or Cancelled tournaments don't count.

**Q: Can I create more tournaments if I hit my limit?**
A: You'll need to complete/cancel existing tournaments or upgrade to a higher tier.

**Q: What's the Multi-Scorer feature?**
A: It allows you to designate other users to score matches in your tournament. Free tier must self-score, Pro tier allows up to 3 scorers.

**Q: What tournament formats are available?**
A: Free tier: Round-robin only. Pro tier (all payment options): Round-robin, Single-elimination knockout, and Hybrid (round-robin → knockout).

**Q: Can I upgrade mid-tournament?**
A: Yes! If you hit a limit (participants, scorers, etc.), you can upgrade immediately and continue your tournament.

3. Click "Upgrade" to view plans

5. New features activate immediately!
